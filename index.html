Credit Requirements
⚠️ Better Earth specific. Credit requirements vary by installer. These apply to Better Earth financer options only. Sunrun not included.
Goodleap TPO · PPA/Lease
CA ONLY ›
Min Credit Score — 680+
FICO Tier 1 — 680+
Initial Check — Soft pull (no hard inquiry)
Bureau — Highest of 3 bureaus
Credit Freeze — Unfreeze all 3 bureaus
DTI — Not evaluated
Non-US Citizen — Yes, SSN required (no ITIN)
Co-Applicant — Allowed. Does NOT need to be on title or live in home. 1 borrower must be on title.
Bankruptcies — Ch.13: discharged 1+ yr. All else: 2+ yrs
Foreclosure — OK if 4+ years ago
Credit Expires — 180 days. No extension.
System Buyout — Anytime after year 5
States — CA Only
Apply Via — Aurora Proposal
Spanish Agreement — ✅ Available
Note — Must have HIS license in CA
Palmetto LightReach TPO · PPA/Lease
CA & FL ›
Min Credit Score — 670 (CA) · 680 (FL)
FICO Tier 1 — 670+
Initial Check — Soft pull (no hard inquiry)
Bureau — Highest of 3 bureaus
Credit Freeze — Unfreeze all 3 bureaus
DTI — Not evaluated
Non-US Citizen — Yes, SSN required (no ITIN)
Co-Applicant — Must be immediate family. 1 must pass credit + title.
Max System Cost — Below $150,000
Bankruptcies — OK if 7+ years ago
Foreclosure — OK if 7+ years ago
Credit Expires — Does not expire. May cancel files after 160+ days without install.
Extension — Yes. Up to 30–60 days. Max 1 extension.
System Buyout — Anytime after year 5
States — CA & FL
Apply Via — Aurora Proposal
Spanish Agreement — ✅ Available
Note — Must be ReCheck Verified
EnFin by Qcells TPO · PPA/Lease
CA & FL ›
Min Credit Score — 620+
FICO Tier 1 — 620+
Initial Check — Soft → Hard after NTP Approval
Bureau — TransUnion
Credit Freeze — Unfreeze TransUnion only
DTI — Not evaluated
Non-US Citizen — Yes, SSN required (no ITIN)
Co-Applicant — Allowed. Does NOT need to be on title or live in home.
Home Sale Transfer — New buyer must meet 630 credit min
Bankruptcies — OK if 3+ years ago
Foreclosure — OK if 3+ years ago
Credit Expires — 180 days. No extension.
System Buyout — At year 5 or year 25
States — CA & FL
Apply Via — Aurora Proposal
Spanish Agreement — ❌ Not available
Note — Must use QCell panels. Must be ReCheck Verified.
Sungage Financial Loan
CA · FL · TX ›
Min Credit Score — 640+
FICO Tiers — T1: 740+ · T2: 700–739 · T3: 640–699
Initial Check — Soft → Hard after install
Bureau — Experian
Credit Freeze — Unfreeze Experian
DTI — None if 700+. 65% max (680–699). 50% max (660–679). 40% max (640–659)
Income Verification — Depends on credit. Required for self-employed.
ITIN Accepted — ❌ No
Co-Applicant — Yes. Both must live in home. One must be on deed.
Loan Limits by Score — 740+: $100k+ · 700: $65k+ · 670–699: $50k+ · 640–659: $7,500–$50k
Min Loan — $7,500 · Max Loan — $150,000
PPW Cap — $5.75/w solar only (CA) · $4.75/w (FL)
Bankruptcies — Must be cleared 2+ years
Foreclosure — Must be cleared 2+ years
Credit Expires — 180 days. No extension.
First Payment — 60 days after install
Pre-Payment Penalty — None if $2,500+. Fee if under $2,500.
States — CA, FL, TX
Spanish Agreement — ❌ Not available
Note — Must have HIS in CA
HomeRun PACE Financing
CA & FL ›
Min Credit Score — N/A (PACE — property-based, not credit-based)
Initial Check — Soft pull (no hard inquiry)
Bureau — TransUnion & Experian
Credit Freeze — Unfreeze one of them
ITIN Accepted — ✅ Yes
DTI — Income must exceed known debts + payment
Co-Applicant — Must be on title or occupying home

PPW Cap — $6.25/w solar only · $10/w solar+battery · $7.50/w with ground mount & MPU. No reroofs.
Bankruptcies — OK if discharged 2+ years (San Diego: 7+ years)
Foreclosure — OK as long as no foreclosure on current home
Payment — Through property tax
First Payment — Dec (signed before 6/30) or following Dec (signed after 7/1)
States — CA & FL
Spanish Agreement — ✅ Available
Note — Must have HIS in CA to sign contracts
